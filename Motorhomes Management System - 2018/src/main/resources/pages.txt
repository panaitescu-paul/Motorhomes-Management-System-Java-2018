			-new customer page elements: firstname, lastname, email, address, phone, ssn
		-motorhomes page: list of motorhomes, "add new" button
			-single motorhome page: details, current rental, current accessories (if current rental is not null)
			-new motorhome page elements: brand, type, model, year, basePrice,
	-auto mechanic: has one page: motorhomes
		-motorhomes page: list of motorhomes, "add new" button
